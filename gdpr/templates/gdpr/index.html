GDPR Policy

At Ethical Gearz, accessible from www.ethicalgearz.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This GDPR Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Ethical Gearz and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our GDPR Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

We are a Data Controller of your information.

Ethical Gearz legal basis for collecting and using the personal information described in this GDPR Policy depends on the Personal Information we collect and the specific context in which we collect the information:

  • Ethical Gearz needs to perform a contract with you
  • You have given Ethical Gearz permission to do so
  • Processing your personal information is in Ethical Gearz legitimate interests
  • Ethical Gearz needs to comply with the law

Ethical Gearz will retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this GDPR Policy. We will retain and use your information to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights. If you wish to be informed what Personal Information we hold about you and if you want it to be removed from our systems, please contact us.

In certain circumstances, you have the following data protection rights:

  • The right to access, update or to delete the information we have on you.
  • The right of rectification.
  • The right to object.
  • The right of restriction.
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent
