Ethical Policy

Ethical Gearz is dedicated to using security testing tools and products ethically. As a responsible member of the security community, we have established the following ethical policy for our customers:

  1. Ethical Use Only: Our products are intended for security testing and auditing and must comply with all local, state, and federal laws. Therefore, we strongly discourage any illegal use of our products.
  2. Informed Consent: Ethical Gearz will not be held responsible for any damages or liabilities resulting from using or misusing our products. By purchasing our products, customers agree to take full responsibility for their actions and use the products legally and ethically.
  3. Professionalism: Ethical Gearz strongly encourages customers to act professionally and ethically when using our products. We do not condone any malicious or harmful actions and will not tolerate unethical behaviour from our customers.
  4. Responsible Disclosure: We urge our customers to adhere to responsible disclosure practices when identifying and reporting security vulnerabilities to others.
  5. Respect for Privacy: Ethical Gearz respects the privacy of all individuals and organizations and will not sell or share any customer information with third parties without prior consent.
  6. Compliance with International Laws: Customers who purchase our products are responsible for complying with all international laws regarding using security testing tools.

Customers agree to adhere to the above ethical policy by using our website and purchasing our products. Any violation of this policy may result in the termination of the customer's account and the refusal of future service.
