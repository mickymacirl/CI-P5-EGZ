Delivery Information Policy

At Ethical Gearz, we strive to provide our customers with the best possible service. That's why we offer a range of delivery options to suit your needs.

Delivery Times

We aim to dispatch all orders within 1-2 business days. Delivery times will vary depending on your location and the delivery option you choose at checkout. Please note that delivery times are estimates only and we cannot guarantee that your order will arrive within the specified timeframes.

Delivery Options

We offer the following delivery options:

  • Standard Delivery: This option usually takes 3-5 business days for delivery within the United States.
  • Express Delivery: This option usually takes 1-2 business days for delivery within the United States.

International Delivery

We offer international delivery to select countries. Delivery times and costs will vary depending on your location and the delivery option you choose at checkout.

Delivery Costs

Delivery costs will vary depending on your location and the delivery option you choose at checkout. Delivery costs will be calculated at checkout and added to your order total.

Delivery Tracking

We provide tracking information for all orders. You will receive an email with a tracking number once your order has been dispatched. You can track your delivery online using the tracking number provided.

Delivery Issues
